Vignan's Institute of Information Technology (VIIT), Visakhapatnam is a centre of excellence in value based education, training, research and consultancy. The institute has been successfully growing and molding the students into complete individuals who are competent energy in meeting global industry challenges. The institute is approved by AICTE, accredited by NAAC and is affiliated to Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University, Kakinada.
The vision of VIIT is to be recognized leader in technical education and shall aim at national excellence by creating competent & socially conscious technical manpower for the current and the future industrial requirements and development of the nation.
Their mission is to fulfill their vision by undertaking research & development in thrust areas, introducing innovative practices of Teaching Learning, continuously collaborating with industry, promoting strong set of ethical values and serving the surrounding region and nation at large.


Campus of Vignan's Institute of Information Technology -

VIIT is located in the industrial city of Visakhapatnam in proximity to important industrial sectors and industries. The college is easily accessible by bus and train and is located near the airport. Vignan’s IIT is built in a sprawling campus with apt infrastructure established in more than one Lakh sq.ft of constructed area.

Placements at Vignan's Institute of Information Technology -

The placement cell at VIIT organizes campus interviews for final year students with industries and business houses of repute from all over India. They prepare students to face campus interviews by arranging training in aptitude tests, group discussions, preparing for technical and HR interviews through professional trainers and VIIT alumni.
